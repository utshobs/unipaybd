UniPay - Unified Interoperable QR Merchant App.

UniPay is an artificial intelligence-based merchant app packed with a lot of features that make payments easy and accessible for business-owners. We are trying to solve all the problems associated with conventional digital banking or payment methods. Our app features include unified payment receive, AI-based loans, microcredit issuing, link payment, and NFC payments etc.

Why You Should Choose UniPay

Every feature is included keeping in mind the needs of a merchant.

Unified QR

Our app uses interoperable single QR codes for merchants to receive from any other payment wallet like bkash, upay, rocket, nagad etc.

Tap On Phone

Merchants can accept payment right on their smartphone having NFC. By which, UniPay ensures zero hardware need and lowest cost to accept payment.

Credit Score

With our Artificial intelligent(AI) model we can measure credit score to detect loan issuing risk.

Sales Analysis

UniPay gives merchants sales Analytics which helps them to grow their business.

Comes With All You Need.

Our app features include unified payment receive, AI-based loans, microcredit issuing, link payment, and NFC payments, which will make transactions more seamless, detect loan issuing risk factors, decrease the credit gap, easy money request and different ways of receiving money.
